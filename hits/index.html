Why the TSU Higher IT School?

Quality

TSU is one of the leading universities in Russia and one of the top global universities, teaching its students following the highest educational practices of the world's leading schools: MIT, Stanford, Carnegie Mellon.

Carrer Opportunities

Our students become world-class developers during their studies. The main principle of our educational program is “learning by doing”. Believing that a programmer learns when it does something on its own, from the first year, we teach our students to develop their own computer game and move on to web development, information security, data science, IoT, etc.

Flexibility

The program is available to any student from anywhere in the world with Internet connection. Our Learning Management System promotes effective learning and communication to help you succeed academically and professionally.

Whant to know more?

Why online?

Be mobile:

By studying in our program you can access course materials from any device anytime and anywhere.

Always stay connected:

Our learning platform is equipped with video calling, synchronous viewing of presentations and text, and student-teacher chat, allowing you to constantly communicate with professors and classmates.

Keep track of your learning process:

Our educational program provides you with full transparency of the learning process and allows you to track your rating formed based on your academic progress. While peer-to-peer review and automatic paper evaluation functions allow you not to doubt the objectivity of your results.
